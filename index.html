InCoM

Intent-Driven Perception and Structured Coordination for Whole-Body Mobile Manipulation

Abstract

We present InCoM, a framework for whole-body mobile manipulation that leverages intent-driven perception and structured coordination to achieve robust performance across diverse tasks. Our approach enables a mobile manipulator to perform complex manipulation tasks in both simulation and real-world environments.
